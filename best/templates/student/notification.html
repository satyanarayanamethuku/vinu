Terms and Conditions
BEST is an online scholarship Exam conducted by Avira Foundation in order to bring out hidden talents.
All Indian National Students with any board (ICSE/CBSE/Respective State Board) of 6th to 10th class are ELIGIBLE to this scholarship test.
There are two types of registrations, online and offline.
For OFFLINE REGISTRATION visit www.ibest.co.in and download offline application. Fill all the fields and attach required documents and send to office address.We are not responsible for any postal or courier DELAYS.
We are NOT RESPONSIBLE for missing application in offline mode.
If there are any missing fields in application and the documents are missed, application will be rejected and send back to your address(For offline, DD charges and postal charges will be paid by you).
In ONLINE REGISTRATION, fill all fields without mistakes and pay the fee within due time.
User ID and Password will be send to your registered mail ID.These will help you to LOGIN into student portal.
After the registration a choice will be given to the participants to answer the mock tests ought before the exam.
Exam mode is ONLINE only. It has to write in allocated time i.e., 90minutes.
There will be a fixed time for each question
We will not be responsible if there is a system error or power failure at the time of the examination. The candidates must be careful (Internet Cafe or any other place with proper internet and power supply).
Students must complete the 120-question multiple-choice exam within the 90-minute time frame allotted for the exam.
Students must not stop the session and then return to it. This is especially important in the online environment where the system will "time-out" and not allow the student or you to reenter the exam site.
Logical reasoning and mathematics are taken into consideration for results (If, the candidates get same score).
In any case, fee is NON REFUNDABLE.
DD or cheque should be taken in favour of Bharat Excellence Scholarship Test which is payable at Madhapur, Hyderabad.
If there is any cancellation of DD or cheque, charges will be paid by the candidate only.
All legal proceedings are subjected to the jurisdiction of Rangareddy court only.
